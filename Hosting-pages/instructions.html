How to Vote

Follow these steps to cast your vote online:

Step 1: Login

Login to the online voting system using your student ID and password.

Step 2: Choose a Candidate

Select the candidate you wish to vote for from the list of Candidates.

Step 3: Review Your Selection

Make sure you have selected the correct candidate before submitting your vote.

Step 4: Submit Your Vote

Click on the "Submit" button to cast your vote. A confirmation message will appear once your vote has been successfully recorded.
